LATHE MACHINE D - EQUIPMENT MANUAL
Model: LM-4000 Series
Equipment ID: E004

TEMPERATURE GUIDELINES
Normal operating temperature range: 45C to 75C
Warning threshold: 80C
Critical threshold: 95C
If temperature exceeds 80C, inspect cooling system immediately.
High temperature is commonly caused by coolant blockage, worn bearings, or excessive cutting speed.
Recommended action: Stop machine, allow 30 minute cooldown, inspect coolant lines.

VIBRATION GUIDELINES
Normal vibration range: 0.5 to 3.5 mm/s
Warning threshold: 5.0 mm/s
Critical threshold: 8.0 mm/s
High vibration indicates worn bearings, spindle imbalance, or loose mounting bolts.
Recommended action: Check bearing condition, inspect spindle alignment, tighten mounting hardware.

PRESSURE GUIDELINES
Normal hydraulic pressure: 60 to 90 PSI
Warning threshold: 100 PSI
Critical threshold: 120 PSI
High pressure indicates blockage in hydraulic lines or failing pressure relief valve.

MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
Daily: Check coolant level, inspect cutting tool condition
Weekly: Lubricate spindle bearings, check belt tension
Monthly: Full bearing inspection, coolant system flush
Every 90 days: Complete overhaul inspection by certified technician

TROUBLESHOOTING
Problem: Machine overheating
Cause: Coolant blockage or low coolant level
Fix: Flush coolant system, refill to recommended level, inspect pump

Problem: Excessive vibration during operation
Cause: Worn spindle bearings
Fix: Replace bearings immediately, recalibrate spindle balance

Problem: Hydraulic pressure spike
Cause: Blocked hydraulic filter
Fix: Replace hydraulic filter, bleed system
